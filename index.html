Our Flagship Journal

Magnalia

The journal of Catholic cultural renewal in Canada

Magnalia — meaning great and wonderful things — brings together the best Catholic writing, scholarship, and art in Canada. Each 60-page issue features essays from leading Catholic thinkers, letters from the field, and beautiful visual art. Published twice yearly.
